# Reviewer Escalation Policy — Internal Operations Manual
# Effective: January 1, 2024 | Owner: CMS Plan Review Operations

## Reviewer Assignment Rules

### JUNIOR Reviewers (handle low-complexity HUMAN_REVIEW cases)
Assign when ALL of the following are true:
- Risk score: 31–50
- First-time submitter OR trusted organization
- No active fraud flags
- Single-state coverage
- Fewer than 3 plans in the submission
- Estimated review time: 30 minutes or less

### SENIOR Reviewers (handle complex or elevated-risk cases)
Assign when ANY of the following are true:
- Risk score: 51–70
- Organization has prior rejections (check risk profiles)
- Multi-state coverage (3+ states)
- Premium outside regional benchmark range
- Missing non-critical fields (contact info gaps, minor compliance questions)
- Estimated review time: more than 30 minutes

### LEAD Reviewer (escalation only — do not assign directly)
Escalate from SENIOR when:
- Risk score > 65 but not high enough for AUTO_REJECT
- Organization is on ELEVATED SCRUTINY list
- Submission involves more than 5 plans
- Reviewer requests second opinion
- Legal or compliance team involvement needed

## SLA Targets

| Reviewer Level | Target Decision Time | Breach Action |
|----------------|---------------------|---------------|
| JUNIOR | 24 hours | Auto-escalate to SENIOR |
| SENIOR | 48 hours | Notify supervisor |
| LEAD | 72 hours | Director review required |
